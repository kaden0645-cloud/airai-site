1. Introduction

AirAI ("we," "us," or "our") provides AI-powered receptionist and phone automation services. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our services or visit airai-site.vercel.app.

2. What Information We Collect

Personal Identifiers: Your name, phone number, email address, and business address when you schedule appointments or contact us.

Call & Communication Data: Call recordings, transcripts, and interaction logs generated when you communicate with our AI agents.

Appointment Data: Scheduled service dates, times, and related service details.

Cookie & Tracking Data: We use standard web cookies and analytics tools to collect data about website visits including IP address, browser type, and pages visited. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

Device & Usage Data: Information about the device and browser used to access our website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: confirm and remind you of scheduled appointments via SMS; operate and improve our AI phone services; respond to customer support inquiries; and comply with legal obligations.

SMS Opt-In: By providing your phone number and interacting with our service, you expressly consent to receive SMS text messages from AirAI. Messages may include appointment confirmations, appointment reminders, and service follow-up messages.

Message Frequency: Message frequency varies. You may receive up to 3–5 messages per appointment.

Message & Data Rates: Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

Opt-Out: You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. You will receive one final confirmation and no further messages will be sent.

Help: Reply HELP for assistance or contact us at kadenchilds19@hotmail.com.

We do not sell, rent, share, or disclose your SMS opt-in data or phone number to any third parties for marketing purposes. Your SMS consent is not transferable. We do not use purchased, rented, or affiliate lead lists.

6. Data Security Practices

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS), access controls, and secure third-party service providers. While we take reasonable precautions, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure.

We may share your data with trusted service providers who help us operate our business (such as CRM platforms, SMS delivery providers, and scheduling tools). You have the right to: access the personal information we hold about you; request correction of inaccurate data; request deletion of your personal data; and unsubscribe from SMS at any time by replying STOP. To exercise these rights contact us at kadenchilds19@hotmail.com.
